Calcualating next Monday's date

To know the days to next Monday you must know:
- the current day of the month
- the current day of the week
- is it the end of the month?
- is it a leap year? (considering February)

Challenge:
- Write code that calculatess next Monday's date
- Use today's day and date to start
- Caclulate the number of days until next Monday
- Consider the end of the month in your calcualtion
- Output the date for next Monday

Example of output:

01_11$./monday
Today is Wednesday, July 7th, 2021
Next Monday will be the 12th of July
