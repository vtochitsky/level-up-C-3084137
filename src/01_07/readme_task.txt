
leapyear()
A single int argument, the year
Return an int TRUE(1) or FALSE(0) 

leap year rules:
1. if the year is evenly divisible by 4, it's a leap year
2. Unless the year is also evenly divisible by 100, in which case it's not a leap year
3. Unless the same year is alos divisible by 400, in which case it is a leap year.
